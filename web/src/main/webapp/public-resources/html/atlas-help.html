Enter Ensembl gene ID(s), gene symbol(s) or keywords to search with genes of interest. Separate them with new lines or spaces. Leave blank to search all annotated genes.
Select experimental factor value(s) to see gene expression profiles for. Leave blank to see expressions in all of them. Factor values are different experimental conditions, e.g. different tissues or cell types.
Enter a minimum expression level (FPKM) cutoff. Only genes with expression above this level will be displayed. The default cutoff is 0.5; to see results for the entire data set, change it to 0. FPKM stands for "fragments per kilobase of transcript per million mapped reads", and is a normalized measure of gene expression.
The histogram shows the number of genes (y-axis) expressed above expression level cutoffs (x-axis) for the chosen factor value(s). You can use it to help decide on a minimum cutoff. Click on a bar to select its expression level as the minimum cutoff.
Toggle display of histogram showing the distribution of expression levels.
Choose a minimum expression level (FPKM) cutoff using the slider. Only genes with expression above this level will be displayed.
The colour gradient represents relative expression level: the more saturated the colour, the higher the expression level is. Click "Display levels" button to see minimum and maximum expression levels.
Tissues studied in this data set are displayed on the anatomogram. Mouseover a gene in the table to highlight the tissue(s) in which it is expressed above the selected minimum expression level. Mouseover tissues in the table to highlight them on the anatomogram.
How are the results sorted? Genes are sorted in order of expression level, from highest to lowest. * If you did not select any factor values, results are ordered so that genes with expression above the chosen cutoff in any one factor value are shown first (with highest expression level first), and then genes with expression above the cutoff in any two factor values, and so on. * If you selected a set of factor values, results are ordered so that genes with expression above the chosen cutoff in all your chosen factor values are shown first (with highest expression level first), followed by genes with expression above the cutoff in all but one of your chosen factor values, followed by genes with expression in all but two of your factor values, and so on.
Only the top 50 genes are displayed on this page; please download your results to see all genes.
A coloured box indicates the gene is expressed under the corresponding factor value(s). The colour saturation of the box indicates relative expression level. To see the exact expression level (FPKM), click on the box, or click the "Display levels" button in the top-left of the table to see them all. Expression levels greater than or equal to 1 are rounded to the nearest whole value. Expression levels less than 1 are rounded to 1 decimal place.
